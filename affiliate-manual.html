⚠️ 6. Trading Risks — READ CAREFULLY

CRITICAL RISK WARNING Investing in financial instruments involves substantial risk of loss. You may lose some or all of your invested capital. The following risks are real and must be understood before using any trading or investment feature.

Market Risk

The value of investments can go down as well as up. Financial markets are influenced by economic conditions, company performance, geopolitical events, and investor sentiment — all of which are unpredictable.

Options Trading Risk

Options are complex financial instruments. An option can expire completely worthless, resulting in a 100% loss of the premium paid. Selling options without full understanding can result in losses exceeding your initial investment.

AI Signal Risk

AI trading signals are generated by algorithms trained on historical data. They cannot predict the future. Signals can be wrong. Always perform your own research before acting on any signal.

Cryptocurrency Risk

Cryptocurrency markets are highly volatile. Assets have historically lost 50-90% of their value in short periods. Warren Buffett has specifically stated that cryptocurrency "basically has no value" — invest with extreme caution.

Your Capital Is At Risk

Only invest money you can afford to lose entirely. Never invest borrowed money. Never invest funds you need for living expenses, emergency savings, or other essential purposes.

🎩 Warren Buffett's First Rule "Rule #1: Never lose money. Rule #2: Never forget Rule #1."

Capital preservation comes before profit. Start small, learn the platform, and scale only with profits — never with money you cannot afford to lose.

Suggested Practices

  • Paper trade (simulate) for 30 days before risking real money
  • Never risk more than 1-2% of your portfolio on a single trade
  • Always set stop-loss orders
  • Diversify across multiple positions and asset classes
  • Review your positions daily and never leave large leveraged positions overnight without a plan

📡 7. AI Trading Signals & TradeAlgo

Once certified, EVICS delivers trading signals from TradeAlgo's premier membership directly to your app. Here is what each signal contains:

  • Asset: The stock, ETF, crypto, or instrument to act on
  • Action: BUY, SELL, or HOLD recommendation
  • Confidence: AI confidence score 0–100%. Only signals above 70% are delivered.
  • Entry Price: Suggested entry price range
  • Exit / Take Profit: Target exit price
  • Stop Loss: Recommended stop loss level to limit downside
  • Timeframe: Signal validity window (1 hour, 4 hours, 1 day)
  • Reasoning: Brief explanation of the trade thesis
Signals Are Informational Only All signals are reviewed against Warren Buffett's investment principles before delivery. Signals that conflict with core value-investing principles will include a Buffett Advisory warning.

Subscribing Your Wallet

To receive trading signals delivered to your specific wallet context, subscribe via Settings → Trading Signals → Subscribe. Select your preferred asset classes and minimum confidence threshold.
