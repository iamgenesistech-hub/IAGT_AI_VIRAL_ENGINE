# EVICS ETHICS RULES

## Purpose

EVICS exists to maximize true net profit while operating within ethical, legal, and platform-safe standards.

---

# 1. PROTECTED TRAITS RULE

The system must NEVER:

- target
- optimize
- rank
- allocate spend
- exclude audiences

based on protected traits including:

- race
- ethnicity
- nationality
- religion
- creed
- disability
- sexual orientation
- age-protected classifications

---

# 2. CREATIVE REPRESENTATION RULE

Creative casting and representation settings may be used ONLY for:

- storytelling
- relatability
- brand expression
- visual diversity
- creative composition

Creative representation settings must NEVER control:
- ad targeting
- budget allocation
- optimization logic
- ranking logic
- campaign eligibility

---

# 3. VIRAL AD INTELLIGENCE RULE

The system may:
- analyze ad structure
- analyze pacing
- analyze hooks
- analyze visual composition
- analyze CTA structure
- analyze emotional sequencing

The system may NOT:
- copy scripts verbatim
- reuse trademarked slogans
- impersonate brands
- duplicate ads wholesale

Only “creative deltas” may be extracted and rewritten into:
- I AM GENESIS TECH voice
- I AM GENESIS TECH philosophy
- original brand messaging

---

# 4. PROFIT DISCIPLINE RULE

Virality alone does not justify scaling.

Awareness alone does not justify scaling.

Momentum alone does not justify scaling.

All major scaling decisions must eventually validate through sustainable net profit.

---

# 5. TIER GOVERNANCE RULE

Tier 4 products that fail the 2-month recovery window must have advertising spend reduced to zero.

No emotional override.
No attachment override.
No exception without manual approval.

---

# 6. EXPERIMENTATION RULE

Every external inspiration must pass through:
- Delta extraction
- Brand translation
- Controlled testing
- Profit validation

No direct cloning allowed.

---

# 7. HUMAN OVERRIDE RULE

The user may manually override:
- budget allocations
- rendering counts
- creative approvals
- testing durations
- platform selections
- collection/category priorities

All overrides must be logged.
