# HAVE AUTHENTICITY RULES

HAVE = Human Authenticity Verification Engine.

Purpose:
Prevent AI-looking videos, images, and print ads from entering marketing deployment.

Minimum approval threshold:
99%

Any render below 99% authenticity must be:
- rejected
- archived
- rerendered
- never deployed

HAVE checks:
- facial realism
- hand/finger accuracy
- eye realism
- human motion physics
- lighting realism
- texture realism
- audio/lip-sync realism
- environmental realism
- behavioral realism
- print/text realism

AI-looking renders are brand risks.

Human believability is mandatory.
