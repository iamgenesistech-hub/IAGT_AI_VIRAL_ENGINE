# EVICS VIRAL INTELLIGENCE RULES

Purpose:
Detect, score, and classify viral ecommerce ad formats for supplement, wellness, beauty, fitness, anti-aging, weight loss, nootropic, testosterone, sea moss, and luxury wellness products.

Core Rules:
1. Never copy copyrighted content directly.
2. Extract structure, not exact creative.
3. Preserve viral psychology, not exact words.
4. Rebuild all concepts using original I AM GENESIS TECH branding.
5. Prioritize supplement-safe language.
6. Score based on views, shares, comments, velocity, engagement, hook strength, CTA clarity, product fit, and conversion potential.
7. Any viral format must pass product matching before recreation.
8. Any recreated ad must pass HAVE authenticity review before deployment.
9. Any ad below minimum viral score is archived for learning only.
10. EVICS must learn from winning hooks, visuals, CTAs, pacing, and product alignment.
