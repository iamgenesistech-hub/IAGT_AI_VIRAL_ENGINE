# DAILY OPERATING PLAYBOOK

## DAILY SYSTEM OBJECTIVE

Maximize TRUE NET PROFIT while maintaining disciplined experimentation, awareness growth, and sustainable scaling.

---

# 1. DAILY PROFIT AUDIT

The Profit Auditor must:

- recalculate net profit for all SKUs
- update ad spend totals
- update refund and return impact
- update margin analysis
- update Weighted Profit Scores

Outputs:
- updated SKU rankings
- updated Top 30 ad rankings
- updated momentum tracking

---

# 2. PRODUCT TIER MONITORING

The Product Tier Manager must:

- monitor all products daily
- track tier movement
- track Tier 4 recovery timers
- detect improvement or decay

Outputs:
- promotion candidates
- pause recommendations
- recovery alerts

---

# 3. CAPITAL ALLOCATION UPDATE

The Capital Allocator must:

- allocate 80% of spend to Top 30 ads
- allocate 20% to Promotion Pool
- shift capital toward profitable momentum
- reduce spend on fatigue or decay

Outputs:
- daily allocation report
- spend increase recommendations
- spend decrease recommendations

---

# 4. CREATIVE INTELLIGENCE SWEEP

The Creative Intelligence Agent must:

- scrape viral ad sources
- analyze WHY ads worked
- extract creative deltas
- identify emotional triggers
- identify visual patterns
- generate recreation opportunities

Outputs:
- viral deconstruction reports
- recreation prompts
- creative opportunity logs

---

# 5. EXPERIMENT GOVERNANCE

The Experiment Governor must:

- monitor active A/B tests
- compare challengers vs baseline
- determine winners and losers
- archive failed experiments
- promote successful formats

Outputs:
- experiment reports
- winner promotion actions
- loser archive actions

---

# 6. LIBRARY STEWARDSHIP

The Library Steward must:

- maintain Top 5 active creatives per SKU
- archive weak creatives
- maintain Fallout Top 100
- maintain Elite Top 20
- update EVICS Render Folder

Outputs:
- active creative library updates
- vault updates
- render ranking updates

---

# 7. EXECUTIVE LOGGING

The Executive Reporter must:

- record all major changes
- update audit logs
- summarize major movements
- prepare executive summaries

Outputs:
- daily report
- operational log
- recommendations
